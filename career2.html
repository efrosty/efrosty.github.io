Computer Hardware Engineering

  • Description: Computer hardware engineers create the physical components that make computers run. They design new hardware systems, test their designs, analyze their test results, and manage manufacturing for completed projects.

  • Average Salary: It ranges from $71,007 to $113,337 depending on experience level

  • Related Careers:
    • Computer Programmer: someone who writes code to generate programs
    • Software Developer: someone who manages the creation of computer programs
    • IT Architect: someone who designs data communication networks
  • Skills Needed:
    • Fluency in these programming languages
      • Verilog
      • C
      • C++
    • Field-Programmable Gate Array
    • Engineering Design
    • Board Design
    • Analytical and Critical Thinking
    • Creativity
    • Communication Skills
    • Problem Solving

  • Experience Required: 6 months to one year of work experience required

  • Education Required: Bachelor's degree for entry-level; master's degree for advancement; doctoral degree for research positions
